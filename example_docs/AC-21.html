AC-21: INFORMATION SHARING

Control Description

The organization:

  1. AC-21a. Facilitates information sharing by enabling authorized users to determine whether access authorizations assigned to the sharing partner match the access restrictions on the information for [Assignment: organization-defined information sharing circumstances where user discretion is required]; and
  2. AC-21b. Employs [Assignment: organization-defined automated mechanisms or manual processes] to assist users in making information sharing/collaboration decisions.

Control Enhancements

  • AC-21 (1) - AUTOMATED DECISION SUPPORT

    The information system enforces information-sharing decisions by authorized users based on access authorizations of sharing partners and access restrictions on information to be shared.

  • AC-21 (2) - INFORMATION SEARCH AND RETRIEVAL

    The information system implements information search and retrieval services that enforce [Assignment: organization-defined information sharing restrictions].

Baselines

MODERATE, HIGH
