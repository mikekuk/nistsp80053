AC-1: ACCESS CONTROL POLICY AND PROCEDURES

Control Description

The organization:

  1. AC-1a. Develops, documents, and disseminates to TEST TEXT:
    1. AC-1a.1. An access control policy that addresses purpose, scope, roles, responsibilities, management commitment, coordination among organizational entities, and compliance; and
    2. AC-1a.2. Procedures to facilitate the implementation of the access control policy and associated access controls; and
  2. AC-1b. Reviews and updates the current:
    1. AC-1b.1. Access control policy [Assignment: organization-defined frequency]; and
    2. AC-1b.2. Access control procedures [Assignment: organization-defined frequency].

Baselines

LOW, MODERATE, HIGH
