AU-8: TIME STAMPS

Control Description

The information system:

  1. AU-8a. Uses internal system clocks to generate time stamps for audit records; and
  2. AU-8b. Records time stamps for audit records that can be mapped to Coordinated Universal Time (UTC) or Greenwich Mean Time (GMT) and meets [Assignment: organization-defined granularity of time measurement].

Control Enhancements

  • AU-8 (1) - SYNCHRONIZATION WITH AUTHORITATIVE TIME SOURCE

    The information system:

    1. AU-8 (1)(a) Compares the internal information system clocks [Assignment: organization-defined frequency] with [Assignment: organization-defined authoritative time source]; and
    2. AU-8 (1)(b) Synchronizes the internal system clocks to the authoritative time source when the time difference is greater than [Assignment: organization-defined time period].

  • AU-8 (2) - SECONDARY AUTHORITATIVE TIME SOURCE

    The information system identifies a secondary authoritative time source that is located in a different geographic region than the primary authoritative time source.

Baselines

LOW, MODERATE, HIGH
