PM-14: TESTING, TRAINING, AND MONITORING

Control Description

The organization:

  1. PM-14a. Implements a process for ensuring that organizational plans for conducting security testing, training, and monitoring activities associated with organizational information systems:
    1. PM-14a.1. Are developed and maintained; and
    2. PM-14a.2. Continue to be executed in a timely manner;
  2. PM-14b. Reviews testing, training, and monitoring plans for consistency with the organizational risk management strategy and organization-wide priorities for risk response actions.
