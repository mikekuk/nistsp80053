CA-1: SECURITY ASSESSMENT AND AUTHORIZATION POLICY AND PROCEDURES

Control Description

The organization:

  1. CA-1a. Develops, documents, and disseminates to [Assignment: organization-defined personnel or roles]:
    1. CA-1a.1. A security assessment and authorization policy that addresses purpose, scope, roles, responsibilities, management commitment, coordination among organizational entities, and compliance; and
    2. CA-1a.2. Procedures to facilitate the implementation of the security assessment and authorization policy and associated security assessment and authorization controls; and
  2. CA-1b. Reviews and updates the current:
    1. CA-1b.1. Security assessment and authorization policy [Assignment: organization-defined frequency]; and
    2. CA-1b.2. Security assessment and authorization procedures [Assignment: organization-defined frequency].

Baselines

LOW, MODERATE, HIGH
