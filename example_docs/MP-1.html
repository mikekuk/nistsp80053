MP-1: MEDIA PROTECTION POLICY AND PROCEDURES

Control Description

The organization:

  1. MP-1a. Develops, documents, and disseminates to [Assignment: organization-defined personnel or roles]:
    1. MP-1a.1. A media protection policy that addresses purpose, scope, roles, responsibilities, management commitment, coordination among organizational entities, and compliance; and
    2. MP-1a.2. Procedures to facilitate the implementation of the media protection policy and associated media protection controls; and
  2. MP-1b. Reviews and updates the current:
    1. MP-1b.1. Media protection policy [Assignment: organization-defined frequency]; and
    2. MP-1b.2. Media protection procedures [Assignment: organization-defined frequency].

Baselines

LOW, MODERATE, HIGH
