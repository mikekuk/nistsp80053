IR-1: INCIDENT RESPONSE POLICY AND PROCEDURES

Control Description

The organization:

  1. IR-1a. Develops, documents, and disseminates to [Assignment: organization-defined personnel or roles]:
    1. IR-1a.1. An incident response policy that addresses purpose, scope, roles, responsibilities, management commitment, coordination among organizational entities, and compliance; and
    2. IR-1a.2. Procedures to facilitate the implementation of the incident response policy and associated incident response controls; and
  2. IR-1b. Reviews and updates the current:
    1. IR-1b.1. Incident response policy [Assignment: organization-defined frequency]; and
    2. IR-1b.2. Incident response procedures [Assignment: organization-defined frequency].

Baselines

LOW, MODERATE, HIGH
