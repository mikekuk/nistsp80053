SA-2: ALLOCATION OF RESOURCES

Control Description

The organization:

  1. SA-2a. Determines information security requirements for the information system or information system service in mission/business process planning;
  2. SA-2b. Determines, documents, and allocates the resources required to protect the information system or information system service as part of its capital planning and investment control process; and
  3. SA-2c. Establishes a discrete line item for information security in organizational programming and budgeting documentation.

Baselines

LOW, MODERATE, HIGH
