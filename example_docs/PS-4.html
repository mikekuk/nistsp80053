PS-4: PERSONNEL TERMINATION

Control Description

The organization, upon termination of individual employment:

  1. PS-4a. Disables information system access within [Assignment: organization-defined time period];
  2. PS-4b. Terminates/revokes any authenticators/credentials associated with the individual;
  3. PS-4c. Conducts exit interviews that include a discussion of [Assignment: organization-defined information security topics];
  4. PS-4d. Retrieves all security-related organizational information system-related property;
  5. PS-4e. Retains access to organizational information and information systems formerly controlled by terminated individual; and
  6. PS-4f. Notifies [Assignment: organization-defined personnel or roles] within [Assignment: organization-defined time period].

Control Enhancements

  • PS-4 (1) - POST-EMPLOYMENT REQUIREMENTS

    The organization:

    1. PS-4 (1)(a) Notifies terminated individuals of applicable, legally binding post-employment requirements for the protection of organizational information; and
    2. PS-4 (1)(b) Requires terminated individuals to sign an acknowledgment of post-employment requirements as part of the organizational termination process.

  • PS-4 (2) - AUTOMATED NOTIFICATION

    The organization employs automated mechanisms to notify [Assignment: organization-defined personnel or roles] upon termination of an individual.

Baselines

LOW, MODERATE, HIGH
