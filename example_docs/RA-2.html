RA-2: SECURITY CATEGORIZATION

Control Description

The organization:

  1. RA-2a. Categorizes information and the information system in accordance with applicable federal laws, Executive Orders, directives, policies, regulations, standards, and guidance;
  2. RA-2b. Documents the security categorization results (including supporting rationale) in the security plan for the information system; and
  3. RA-2c. Ensures that the authorizing official or authorizing official designated representative reviews and approves the security categorization decision.

Baselines

LOW, MODERATE, HIGH
