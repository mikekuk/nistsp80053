PM-15: CONTACTS WITH SECURITY GROUPS AND ASSOCIATIONS

Control Description

The organization establishes and institutionalizes contact with selected groups and associations within the security community:

  1. PM-15a. To facilitate ongoing security education and training for organizational personnel;
  2. PM-15b. To maintain currency with recommended security practices, techniques, and technologies; and
  3. PM-15c. To share current security-related information including threats, vulnerabilities, and incidents.
