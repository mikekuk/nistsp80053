MA-2: CONTROLLED MAINTENANCE

Control Description

The organization:

  1. MA-2a. Schedules, performs, documents, and reviews records of maintenance and repairs on information system components in accordance with manufacturer or vendor specifications and/or organizational requirements;
  2. MA-2b. Approves and monitors all maintenance activities, whether performed on site or remotely and whether the equipment is serviced on site or removed to another location;
  3. MA-2c. Requires that [Assignment: organization-defined personnel or roles] explicitly approve the removal of the information system or system components from organizational facilities for off-site maintenance or repairs;
  4. MA-2d. Sanitizes equipment to remove all information from associated media prior to removal from organizational facilities for off-site maintenance or repairs;
  5. MA-2e. Checks all potentially impacted security controls to verify that the controls are still functioning properly following maintenance or repair actions; and
  6. MA-2f. Includes [Assignment: organization-defined maintenance-related information] in organizational maintenance records.

Control Enhancements

  • MA-2 (1) - RECORD CONTENT

    [Withdrawn: Incorporated into MA-2].

  • MA-2 (2) - AUTOMATED MAINTENANCE ACTIVITIES

    The organization:

    1. MA-2 (2)(a) Employs automated mechanisms to schedule, conduct, and document maintenance and repairs; and
    2. MA-2 (2)(b) Produces up-to date, accurate, and complete records of all maintenance and repair actions requested, scheduled, in process, and completed.
