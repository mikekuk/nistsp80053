AC-8: SYSTEM USE NOTIFICATION

Control Description

The information system:

  1. AC-8a. Displays to users [Assignment: organization-defined system use notification message or banner] before granting access to the system that provides privacy and security notices consistent with applicable federal laws, Executive Orders, directives, policies, regulations, standards, and guidance and states that:
    1. AC-8a.1. Users are accessing a U.S. Government information system;
    2. AC-8a.2. Information system usage may be monitored, recorded, and subject to audit;
    3. AC-8a.3. Unauthorized use of the information system is prohibited and subject to criminal and civil penalties; and
    4. AC-8a.4. Use of the information system indicates consent to monitoring and recording;
  2. AC-8b. Retains the notification message or banner on the screen until users acknowledge the usage conditions and take explicit actions to log on to or further access the information system; and
  3. AC-8c. For publicly accessible systems:
    1. AC-8c.1. Displays system use information [Assignment: organization-defined conditions], before granting further access;
    2. AC-8c.2. Displays references, if any, to monitoring, recording, or auditing that are consistent with privacy accommodations for such systems that generally prohibit those activities; and
    3. AC-8c.3. Includes a description of the authorized uses of the system.

Baselines

LOW, MODERATE, HIGH
