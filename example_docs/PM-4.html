PM-4: PLAN OF ACTION AND MILESTONES PROCESS

Control Description

The organization:

  1. PM-4a. Implements a process for ensuring that plans of action and milestones for the security program and associated organizational information systems:
    1. PM-4a.1. Are developed and maintained;
    2. PM-4a.2. Document the remedial information security actions to adequately respond to risk to organizational operations and assets, individuals, other organizations, and the Nation; and
    3. PM-4a.3. Are reported in accordance with OMB FISMA reporting requirements.
  2. PM-4b. Reviews plans of action and milestones for consistency with the organizational risk management strategy and organization-wide priorities for risk response actions.
