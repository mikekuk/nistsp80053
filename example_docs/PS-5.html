PS-5: PERSONNEL TRANSFER

Control Description

The organization:

  1. PS-5a. Reviews and confirms ongoing operational need for current logical and physical access authorizations to information systems/facilities when individuals are reassigned or transferred to other positions within the organization;
  2. PS-5b. Initiates [Assignment: organization-defined transfer or reassignment actions] within [Assignment: organization-defined time period following the formal transfer action];
  3. PS-5c. Modifies access authorization as needed to correspond with any changes in operational need due to reassignment or transfer; and
  4. PS-5d. Notifies [Assignment: organization-defined personnel or roles] within [Assignment: organization-defined time period].

Baselines

LOW, MODERATE, HIGH
